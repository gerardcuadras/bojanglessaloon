FAQS

Do you accept reservations?

We're happy to accommodate your reservation! To secure your table, you can either give us a call at 0402 992 722 or conveniently book online through our website calendar. Our reservation system allows you to choose your preferred date, time, and party size. Please note that for larger groups or special occasions, we recommend making a reservation in advance to guarantee your spot.

Can you accommodate food allergies?

At Bojangles, we understand the importance of catering to dietary restrictions. We can accommodate a variety of food allergies and intolerances. When making your reservation, please inform our staff of any specific dietary requirements. Our kitchen team is trained to handle these requests with care and attention. We recommend discussing your needs with our staff to ensure a safe and enjoyable dining experience.

Do you host special events?

Absolutely! We love to create a lively atmosphere for our guests. We regularly host exciting events like karaoke nights, trivia challenges, and beer pong tournaments. These events are a great way to unwind, have fun, and socialize with friends. Keep an eye on our website and social media for upcoming events and schedules. We also offer the option to host private events for special occasions. Contact our events coordinator for more information on customizing your event.
